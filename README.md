🎓 **University of Technology Sydney — Bangladeshi Student Society**
🌏 A vibrant student community dedicated to **celebrating Bangladeshi culture**, fostering friendships, and creating unforgettable memories for students in Sydney.
💻 Our GitHub is home to **open-source projects, digital archives, and cultural resources** — built by students, for students.

---

## ✨ About Us

UTSBDSOC isn’t just a society — it’s a family.
We exist to:

* 🇧🇩 **Celebrate Bangladeshi heritage** — through food, music, art, language, and festivals
* 🤝 **Build connections** — welcoming students from all backgrounds to experience our culture
* 🎉 **Host inclusive events** — from cultural nights and competitions to casual meetups and workshops
* 📸 **Preserve our history** — archiving event media, stories, and milestones for future generations

---

## 🧩 Our GitHub Space

Here you’ll find:

* 🌐 **Website repositories** — powering our official site & event pages
* 📂 **Media archives** — preserving photos, videos, and memories
* 📚 **Cultural resources** — educational content about Bangladesh for students and the public
* 🛠 **Open-source tools** — built by our tech team and contributors

---

## 🤝 How You Can Get Involved

You don’t have to be a coder to contribute! We welcome:

* 🖋 **Writers & editors** — improve event descriptions, blog posts, and captions
* 🎨 **Designers** — create posters, banners, and social media visuals
* 💻 **Developers** — help maintain or enhance our website and digital projects
* 📸 **Photographers & videographers** — capture our events and community moments
* 💡 **Idea-makers** — propose new events, resources, or tech initiatives

📄 Check our [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) for detailed guidelines.
